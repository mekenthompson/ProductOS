# Tempo: the Worked Example

**Tempo** is a fictional team-scheduling product for multi-location service
operators (gyms, dental groups, care providers). It exists to give every
ProductOS template a filled-in example next to the blank shape, one company,
carried through the whole method. Everything about Tempo, its customers, and
its numbers is invented.
