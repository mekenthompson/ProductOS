# Strategy Example: Tempo

This is a worked example of the [Strategy template](../../templates/strategy.md) for a fictional team-scheduling product called **Tempo**. The customers — FitClub, BrightSmile, CityCare — are fictional. The jobs, market pressures, and competitive moves are illustrative.

Read this alongside [guides/strategy-as-code.md](../../guides/strategy-as-code.md), which explains the method behind the template. The two decision records in `decisions/` show how the strategy evolved over the period and why — a realistic direction change prompted by a competitor move.
